02 · The Problem

Campus expertise is invisible

Thousands of students hold valuable skills — coding, music, design, languages — but there's no structured way to connect, teach, or learn from each other.

🔍

No discovery layer

Skills stay undiscovered. WhatsApp groups and notice boards are unstructured and unreliable.

⚡

No incentive to teach

Students with skills have no motivation to share them — time investment with zero reward.

📉

No quality assurance

Even when connections form, there's no rating, accountability, or follow-through mechanism.

06 · AI Matchmaking & Scheduling

Matching made intelligent

The AI assistant doesn't just list available teachers. It deeply analyses compatibility and auto-schedules everything — so students focus on learning, not logistics.

Skill gap analysis

Maps what the learner needs vs. what each teacher offers at which level

Compatibility scoring

Learning style, pace, schedule overlap — all factored in before suggesting a match

Auto-scheduling

Reads both calendars, proposes slots, confirms — zero back-and-forth needed

Feedback loop

Every session rating improves future match quality across the platform
